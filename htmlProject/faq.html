The PlayStation 5 supports gaming at up to 4K resolution at 120 frames per second (FPS).

The PlayStation 5 utilizes a custom SSD (Solid State Drive) for its internal storage, providing faster load times and smoother gameplay experiences.

The PlayStation 5 is equipped with 16 GB of GDDR6 RAM, allowing for seamless multitasking and high-performance gaming.

The DualSense controller introduces haptic feedback and adaptive triggers, which offer more immersive gameplay experiences by providing tactile sensations that correspond to in-game actions.

The PlayStation 5 supports Tempest 3D AudioTech, which aims to provide immersive spatial audio for a more realistic gaming experience.
