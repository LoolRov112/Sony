System Operation

You can turn on the PS5 in two ways, the first way is by using the power button located on the lower front side of the console itself and the second way is through a synced controller. The controller has a PS symbol button located in the lower center. With the same button you can turn the console on/off, return to the home screen, and use the quick menu. The controller has 4 face buttons, a Dpad (used for navigating in the UI, 2d movement, and some in game functions), 2 analog sticks (used for 3d movement) 4 shoulder buttons (2 bumpers and 2 adaptive triggers) and 3 utility buttons (options, share, and PS button).


General Maintenance

The PS5 is easy to clean and maintain. Under the left side piece there are 3 ports for a vacuum to suck out the dust from the APU cooler. Under the right wing you have the disc drive, fan and M.2 SSD connector. You can remove the fan to clean and replace it, you can manually eject stuck discs from the disc drive by unscrewing a small screw that is attached to the disc drive.
