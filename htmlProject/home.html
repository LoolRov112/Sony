General Knowledge

The Sony PlayStation 5 or in short PS5 is a video game console developed by Sony Interactive Entertainment and launched on November 12, 2020. The PS5 has two models: an optical disc drive version with an ultra-HD Blu-ray disc drive and the Digital version which lacks the drive and is priced lower, targeting users who prefer to buy and download games digitally. The PS5 display up to 120 FPS (frames per second) and can reach a resolution of 8K. The PS5 has a custom 7NM 3.6 GHz AMD CPU and a GPU that has 36 compute units which is capable of a peak performance of 10 teraflops. The PS5 connects to the internet via a gigabit LAN cable or through WIFI. The PS5 has 825 GB of storage and you can add more storage by installing an NVMe M.2 SSD.
